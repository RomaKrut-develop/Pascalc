USAGE:

	To input numbers use '1234567890' on keyboard, or on keypad.
	To control with operators '+' and '*':
		hold shift and press '=' to input '+',
		hold shift and press '8' to input '*',
		Or just use keypad.
	To control with operator '/', use only keypad.
	To control with operator '-', press '_-' or just use keypad.
	In this program, you cannot delete numbers by pressing BACKSPACE.
	To clean number input, use keyboard 'c'.
